# Getting Started with LightFocus

LightFocus is a **solar-system task manager** that visualises your workload as planets orbiting a sun.
The closer a planet orbits, the more urgent or important that task is.

---

## 1. Create Your First Task

Press **N** on your keyboard or click the **New Task** button (top-right).

Fill in:
- **Title** — the planet's name
- **Priority** — Critical / High / Medium / Low / None
- **Complexity** — 1 to 5 (affects planet size)
- **Due Date** — optional, but adds urgency to the bullseye rank
- **Color** — pick a colour for your planet

Click **Create** and your planet appears in orbit.

---

## 2. Understand the Bullseye Rank

Every task gets an automatic rank from **1 (Critical)** to **7 (Minimal)** based on
priority, complexity, and time-to-deadline. Rank 1 tasks sit on the innermost orbit; rank 7 tasks float on the outermost.

| Rank | Label     | Distance from sun |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| 1    | Critical  | Innermost orbit   |
| 2    | Very High |                   |
| 3    | High      |                   |
| 4    | Medium    |                   |
| 5    | Low       |                   |
| 6    | Very Low  |                   |
| 7    | Minimal   | Outermost orbit   |

---

## 3. Add Subtasks (Moons)

Open a task and add subtasks. Each subtask orbits its parent planet as a **moon**, spinning in 3D.
Completing a subtask turns its moon **green**.

---

## 4. Chat with ARIA-7

Click the **AI chat bubble** (bottom-right) to talk to ARIA-7, your AI productivity companion.
She remembers your preferences, moods, and goals across sessions to give contextual advice.

---

## 5. Customise Your Experience

Go to **Settings** (⚙️ icon) to:
- Rename ARIA-7 and change her personality
- Set your work schedule, motivation type, and communication style
- View and manage ARIA-7's memory about you
- Adjust bullseye ranking rules
